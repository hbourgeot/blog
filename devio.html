Dev Challenges

Are you just starting out in web development and have no idea what to do or how to polish your coding skills? Here is a website that might help you.

Dev Challenges: real-life coding Challenges

The website have three sections of projects that you can develop and share with the community of that website. The solutions you make, will be shared with all users who use that website.

From left to right, we have the responsive web challenges, there are the front-end developer challenges, this already involves a bit of JS, as there is a project that is to make a website that has the purpose of showing the weather, and finally, the fullstack challenges if you want to learn database and web design. When you finish a section, you can get a certificate.

To do the challenges, they give you the images you need, regarding the delivery time, there is no deadline, you can take as long as you want, and to upload your solution you just need to follow certain steps indicated on the site, at the end of the post you can access their site.
